Abdullahi Qasim, hailing from Nairobi, Kenya, emerged as a talented soccer player from a young age. Born with a passion for the sport, he kicked off his journey at the age of 6, marking the start of his soccer career. His skills flourished, and by the time he was 10, he was already playing for the under 12 team, Al-hilal, achieving victory in a significant cup. After his time with Al-hilal, he transitioned to school teams during his middle and high school years. In 2014, Abdullahi Qasim's life took a transformative turn when he arrived in the United States after undergoing the refugee process. He continued his soccer journey on American soil, becoming a vital player for the Ohio United FC senior team. Prior to this, he showcased his prowess as a member of Hilaac FC, a Columbus-based team, contributing significantly to their success from 2015 to 2019. Despite a setback due to a knee surgery in 2019, Abdullahi's determination endured, leading him to join Ohio United FC and subsequently claim victory in four trophies with the team.
